Cells


The range of services provided by the students varies greatly. Of the plethora of activities, we undertake a few major that include teaching of school students, who cannot afford a good education, both inside and outside the campus, spreading social awareness through lectures, debates and organizing blood donation and first-aid camps. Our work also includes setting up of small-scale industries for the uplift of nearby rural areas. To organise such multifarious activities, NSS has been divided into a number of cells.



AWARENESS AND ENVIRONMENT CELL


Having the vision to awaken the masses, the awareness cell at NSS IIT Roorkee, works to spread alertness related to various social issues in the campus as well as in the vicinity of the campus. Creativity and simplicity are its tools to disperse cognizance on topics like NREGA, Polio, Blood Donation etc. by means of skits, popularly known as ‘Nukkad nataks’ which are written, practiced and then performed on streets so as to attract a large audience and successfully entertain as well as apprise the common people. The zeal to reach and spread information to the masses will never stop.

DRAFTING AND PLANNING CELL


An Integral part of the NSS team, the Drafting and Planning Cell assumes the duty of meeting the monetary requirements of the various activities that are conducted under the canopy of NSS. It includes contacting interested and motivated people who are willing to do their part for their beloved country. Raising funds, their budgeting into various channels, channelizing the funds smoothly, mainting a proper database are the technical aspects of this cell. The non technical aspects include creating a proper socio economic portrayal of rural Indian Society through various surveys and carrying out research and development activities to improve the financial condition of the destitute that includes woman emancipation and creation of part time unskilled labour jobs.

EVENT MANAGEMENT AND PROMOTIONS CELL


The Event Management and Promotions cell is amongst the most active cells of NSS, IIT Roorkee. The EMP cell shoulders the responsibility of promoting and managing the central activities. The cell looks after the planning and conduction of the various NSS guest talks, other events like cloth collection drive, Prabhat Pheri etc. and most importantly, the blood donation camp organised on a huge scale that spawned over a thousand units of donated blood. EMP cell manages activities ranging from inviting and accommodation of our guests to the promotions. Thus the cell is latched to the central NSS office and is an integral part of all the central events happening at NSS.

MODEL VILLAGE CELL


In order to bridge the gap between urban and rural India, Model Village Cell, NSS IIT Roorkee strives to aid the people in the vicinity of Roorkee by employing a hands-on approach in solving issues like illiteracy, bad water quality and lack of health care etc. The cell members regularly travel to villages to conduct surveys and then set out to systematically solve the residents’ problems. Be it even providing basic sanitation to every home or conducting sports events for the children the ever zealous members of the model cell always excel. The cell members attack the problem at the grass root level by providing free education to the children in the village. We aim to create a ‘model village’, one which is not only culturally and economically advanced but also self-sustaining and the efforts won’t stop until this dream is realised.

NAVODAYA CELL


Navodaya cell functions to realise the dream of those students who are willing to study but are unable just because of their socio-economic conditions. JNV’s (Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas) is a group of schools, initiated by Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India to educate such talented students who might have otherwise remained unnoticed. Team Navodaya acts as a pathway for these underprivileged students to come out of their grey zone and dream for a better tomorrow. The students are taught mathematics and logical reasoning, which constitute a major part of the Navodaya entrance questionnaire. Children from nearby villages attend the classes with great zeal and enthusiasm. Navodaya Cell is credited as the most successful and the cell with most regularized activity of teaching village kids.

PRAGATI CELL


Living for oneself is a deed which most of the persons pursue, but the quality of living for others and serving humanity is possessed only by a true human being. The PRAGATI cell of NSS, IIT Roorkee is one such initiative to spread the message of social service and service to mankind. Though comparatively new to the other sections of NSS, this cell has taken immense strides in doing social good, both in and out of the campus. It has collected data about the demographic and economic status of the villages simultaneously helping the villagers to know about the various beneficiary government schemes. Surveys are mainly done in Telliwala village neighbouring the campus. The cell members are also involved in imparting education to children of Telliwala village on a weekly basis. Thus Pragati cell is an effort to work for our fellow human beings and make the society a place worth living.

PRERNA CELL


Prerna cell is one of the most vibrant cells of NSS, IIT Roorkee. It strives to alleviate the needy and underprivileged sections of the society. It focuses on teaching poor children from nearby areas who cannot afford proper tuitions due to lack of substantial income. It emphasizes not only on the educational aspect but also on the overall development of the students. The classes are held at ABN School, IIT Roorkee. Apart from regular teaching activities, it is also responsible for the successful conduct of extra-curricular activities like Sports Day, Cultural Programmes, Fun Activities, Art Activities, and Science Workshops etc. are some, out of the plethora of activities conducted by Prerna Cell.

RT EDUCATION CELL


RT education cell has been a gratified part of NSS IITR since its creation, thanks to its selfless service to society via the most important tool of education by conducting classes for training deprived children of Khanjarpur village. Not restricting itself to academics only, the cell also focuses on the extracurricular aspects by organizing drawing and sport competitions, talent search exams etc. to attract more aspirants and rewarding them for their performances. The different motives of cell can be united into one motive of breaking the shackles of pauperism through the one and only mode of edification.

TECHNOCREATIVE CELL


Techno creative cell of NSS was established in order to document and keep track of the activities of the other eight cells of NSS and thereby an active part of every event/activity of NSS. The cell functions with four sub-cells namely Editorial, Graphic Design, Web Design and Photography. Editorial sub-cell documents the activities of the other cells of NSS and forming individual documents for the annual report. The volunteers cover both on-campus and off-campus events of NSS. Design sub-cell creates designs posters, presentations, etc. for various events of NSS. Photography sub-cell was created last year in Techno creative with an aim of capturing the expressions and visuals of the various events organized by the other cells of NSS. Photography and Editorial complement each other. Whereas the web design sub-cell, restarted from this year, will maintain the web-site, blog and other on-line activities of NSS.
